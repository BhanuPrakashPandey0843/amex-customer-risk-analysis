## One Important Enhancement

What I've outlined is already at an industry standard, but if our goal is **Top 15 out of 15,000**, I propose one refinement:

We should work in **Agile sprints** rather than simply "completing phases." Each phase will be broken into small, reviewable tasks with clear acceptance criteria. After every sprint, we'll review the outputs, challenge our own assumptions, and only then move to the next phase. This iterative approach helps ensure that by the time we reach the executive deck, every chart, KPI, and recommendation has been validated and refined—not just generated. That level of rigor is what will make this project stand out from the vast majority of submissions.
