# Executive KPI Framework
---

## Date Prepared
June 27, 2026

---

## Overview
This document defines all enterprise-grade strategic KPIs for the American Express CFPB Risk Intelligence Analysis.

---

## KPI Inventory

---

### 1. Complaint Volume
| KPI Field | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| KPI Name | Complaint Volume |
| Definition | Total number of complaints received in a given period |
| Formula | COUNT(Complaint ID) |
| Business Purpose | Measures overall complaint scale |
| Interpretation | Higher = more customer dissatisfaction; Lower = better performance |
| Executive Usage | Track overall complaint trends over time; Set volume reduction targets |

---

### 2. Complaint Growth
| KPI Field | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| KPI Name | Complaint Growth |
| Definition | Percentage change in complaint volume vs previous comparable period |
| Formula | ((Current Period Volume / Prior Period Volume) - 1) * 100 |
| Business Purpose | Measures acceleration/deceleration of complaints |
| Interpretation | Positive = worsening trends; Negative = improving trends |
| Executive Usage | Identify emerging issues with rapid growth |

---

### 3. Complaint Share
| KPI Field | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| KPI Name | Complaint Share |
| Definition | Percentage of total industry complaints for a specific product/issue that are about American Express |
| Formula | (Amex Complaints for Product/Issue / Total Industry Complaints for Product/Issue) * 100 |
| Business Purpose | Benchmarks Amex's performance relative to peers |
| Interpretation | Lower = Amex outperforms peers; Higher = Amex underperforms peers |
| Executive Usage | Identify products/issues where Amex is lagging/leading |

---

### 4. Product Risk Index
| KPI Field | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| KPI Name | Product Risk Index |
| Definition | Composite score combining complaint volume, growth, and severity for a product |
| Formula | (Normalized Volume * 0.4) + (Normalized Growth * 0.3) + (Normalized Severity * 0.3) |
| Business Purpose | Identifies highest-risk products |
| Interpretation | 1–10 scale; Higher = higher risk |
| Executive Usage | Prioritize product improvements |

---

### 5. Issue Severity Index
| KPI Field | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| KPI Name | Issue Severity Index |
| Definition | Composite score combining complaint volume, growth, and impact for an issue |
| Formula | (Normalized Volume * 0.4) + (Normalized Growth * 0.3) + (Normalized Impact * 0.3) |
| Business Purpose | Identifies highest-severity issues |
| Interpretation | 1–10 scale; Higher = more severe |
| Executive Usage | Prioritize issue resolution |

---

### 6. Company Risk Score
| KPI Field | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| KPI Name | Company Risk Score |
| Definition | Overall risk score for American Express vs peer companies |
| Formula | Composite of all product and issue risk indices |
| Business Purpose | Benchmarks overall performance |
| Interpretation | 1–10 scale; Lower = better performance |
| Executive Usage | Track overall performance vs peers over time |

---

### 7. Complaint Concentration
| KPI Field | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| KPI Name | Complaint Concentration |
| Definition | Percentage of complaints in the top N issues/products |
| Formula | (Top N Complaint Volume / Total Volume) * 100 |
| Business Purpose | Measures how concentrated complaints are; indicates systemic vs isolated issues |
| Interpretation | Higher = more systemic issues; Lower = more spread out |
| Executive Usage | Determine if issues are isolated or require systemic fixes |

---

### 8. Response Effectiveness
| KPI Field | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| KPI Name | Response Effectiveness |
| Definition | Percentage of complaints closed with explanation or monetary relief |
| Formula | (Closed with Explanation + Closed with Monetary Relief) / Total Closed Complaints * 100 |
| Business Purpose | Measures quality of complaint resolution |
| Interpretation | Higher = better resolution quality |
| Executive Usage | Identify products/issues with poor resolution |

---

### 9. Timeliness Rate
| KPI Field | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| KPI Name | Timeliness Rate |
| Definition | Percentage of complaints responded to on time |
| Formula | (Timely Responses / Total Responses) * 100 |
| Business Purpose | Measures operational response speed |
| Interpretation | Higher = faster, more compliant responses |
| Executive Usage | Track operational performance; Identify bottlenecks |

---

### 10. Executive Priority Score
| KPI Field | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| KPI Name | Executive Priority Score |
| Definition | Composite score combining impact and urgency for recommendations |
| Formula | (Impact Score * 0.7) + (Urgency Score * 0.3) |
| Business Purpose | Prioritizes recommendations for leadership |
| Interpretation | 1–10 scale; Higher = higher priority |
| Executive Usage | Focus leadership attention on highest-priority items |
