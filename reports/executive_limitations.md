# Executive Limitations
---

## Decisions This Dataset CAN Support
1. Relative performance benchmarking between American Express and competitors
2. Identification of high-volume complaint categories by product/issue
3. Trend analysis of complaint volumes over time
4. Assessment of complaint response timeliness and quality
5. Geographic analysis of complaint patterns
6. Prioritization of improvement opportunities

---

## Decisions This Dataset CANNOT Support
1. Direct market share calculations (complaint volume ≠ market share)
2. Customer satisfaction measurement (complaints only measure dissatisfaction)
3. Financial impact quantification (no revenue data)
4. Individual customer-level analysis (no PII)
5. Predictive modeling of future complaints (limited historical data)
6. Root cause analysis of operational failures (no operational data)
7. Competitor pricing strategy (no pricing data)
8. Employee performance metrics (no HR data)

---

## Potential Bias
1. **Selection Bias**: Complaints are self-selected and not representative of all customers
2. **Reporting Bias**: Customers with severe issues are more likely to complain
3. **Channel Bias**: Complaint channel may impact likelihood of escalation
4. **Temporal Bias**: Data covers only Jan 2025–Mar 2026; may not reflect long-term trends
5. **Competitor Bias**: Different companies may have different complaint propensities

---

## Sampling Limitations
- No random sampling
- Non-response bias (only complaints, not satisfied customers)
- Limited time horizon (15 months)
- No information about customer tenure or lifetime value

---

## Regulatory Limitations
- Data is for educational/assessment purposes only
- Not for regulatory reporting to CFPB
- Cannot be used to make formal legal claims
- All analysis must be kept confidential per project guidelines

---

## Business Limitations
- No data on customer acquisition channels
- No data on customer profitability
- No data on product usage patterns
- No data on competitor responses or improvements
- No data on customer retention after complaints

---

## Data Limitations
- No complaint narratives
- No data on complaint outcomes after initial response
- No information about complaint severity
- No complaint resolution time data beyond binary "timely" flag
- No customer demographic information
- No data on merchant or acquirer side

---

## Analytical Limitations
- Correlation does not imply causation
- Limited historical data prevents sophisticated time series analysis
- No external benchmarks (industry averages not available)
- No multivariate testing capabilities
- Cannot quantify financial ROI of improvements
